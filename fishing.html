How to fishing in Taman Syahdu

Taman Syahdu has a lake covers 36 acres where you can fish. The fishing here can sometimes be rather slow so take a bottle or two of bait flavouring with you to improve your chances. They claim they have giant catfish up to 50kg

To fish in Taman Syahdu lake, you need to have a permission letter from the Park Management.

To get permission letter, go to our office and you will be given a form to fill

The fish you caught need to be released back into the lake to preserve the ecosystem

Price

Free

Operating Hours

8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Additional Information

You need to bring your own fishing gear and bait because we don't provide them. Also for bait, we need to know what kind of bait you're using when filling the form before fishing, you cannot use smelly bait like chicken intestine. This is to make sure there's no smell polution and affect other people that enjoying their time in our lake

Our lake staff will monitor fishing area from time to time to help you. Also be informed that the fish you caught need to be released back into the river. Here is fisherman's code that you should follow

  • Keep only the fished needed and within creel and size limits.
  • Do not pollute - Properly dispose of trash. Mono fishing line can be a disaster for wildlife.
  • Sharpen angling and boating skills.
  • Observe angling and boating safety regulations.
  • Respect other anglers' rights.
  • Observe park rules and regulations and respect private property owners' rights.
  • Pass on knowledge and angling skills. Take a kid fishing!!
  • Support local conservation efforts.
  • Never stock fish or plants into public water.
  • Promote the sport of angling.

Please be mindful that we also provide kayak service, therefore you only allowed to fish at certain area where there is no boat around, this is for safety reason and your comfort when fishing
