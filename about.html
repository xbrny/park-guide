Taman Syahdu Park Guide

Welcome to Taman Syahdu Park, a safe, family-friendly park in Malaysia! In Taman Syahdu visitors will get to experience a green setting and view the flora and fauna that inhabit the man-made environment.The park also offers facilities such as cycling trails, fishing lake, and kayaks.

Fishing Cycling Kayak

Fishing

Taman Syahdu has a lake covers 36 acres where you can fish. The fishing here can sometimes be rather slow so take a bottle or two of bait flavouring with you to improve your chances. They claim they have giant catfish up to 50kg

To fish in Taman Syahdu lake, you need to have a permission letter from the Park Management.

Cycling

Taman Syahdu is one of the famous cycling spots in KL with its 9-foot-wide pathway and stunning landscape. The best way to enjoy its beauty is to go cycling around the man-made lake while enjoying the refreshing lakeside breezes.

Make sure you have a good breakfast before coming here to cycle as the terrain here is quite hilly and challenging

Kayak

Searching for a place for kayak, here in Taman Syahdu you can kayak in our 36 acres lake and enjoy the breeze in kayak boat and scenery, kayak-ing here is not as rough or challenging as normal kayak but you can experience kayak boat first hand
