About Park

Welcome to Taman Syahdu Park, a safe, family-friendly park in Malaysia! In Taman Syahdu visitors will get to experience a green setting and view the flora and fauna that inhabit the man-made environment. Our park also offers facilities such as cycling trails, fishing lake, kayak area, as well as bicycles for visitors to better enjoy the sights of the wetland habitats.
